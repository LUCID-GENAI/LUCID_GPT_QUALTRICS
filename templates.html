QSF Templates

Before these Qualtrics templates will function properly, researchers need to complete a short one-time setup process. Full instructions are available in the Notion configuration guide, which walks you through:

  1. Getting an OpenAI API key
  2. Creating a GitHub account
  3. Connecting your GitHub to Vercel
  4. Deploying the LUCID serverless function and obtaining its web address

Once these steps are completed, you can download and launch any of the templates below in Qualtrics.

Starter Template

This template allows survey participants to engage in a single back-and-forth conversation with a GPT-powered chatbot inside a Qualtrics survey.

  • Context: Conversation with Air Canada chatbot.
  • Source: Study 1 in Garvey and Blanchard (2025).

    • Download QSF Preview Study Video Guide

    Between-subject Manipulation in Conversation

    This template allows researchers to randomly allocate survey participants to engage in one of two GPT-powered chatbot inside a Qualtrics survey.

  • Context: Conversation with American Airlines virtual agent. Instructions manipulate the tone of the agent (causual vs. formal).
  • Source: Study 2 in Garvey and Blanchard (2025).
