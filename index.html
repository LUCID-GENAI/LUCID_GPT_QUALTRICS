Get Started with LUCID

LUCID is a lightweight Python toolkit that allows you to embed OpenAI chatbots directly into Qualtrics surveys. Perfect for survey researchers and behavioral scientists looking to add interactive, AI-driven responses to their instruments.

Try it (fresh produce interview)

This project is open source. You're welcome to explore, contribute, or adapt it to your own needs.
